Conclusion


Bulma and NodeJS make a great pair for front- and back-end development. Working with both has been a great learning experience, and the two frameworks are easy to learn, even if one has no prior experience with them.

Our experience with Bulma

Our experience with Bulma


Using Bulma, creating good-looking dynamic webpages was a breeze.
Bulma is very easy to install and set up, and being flexbox-based means it is very customizable.

Our experience with NodeJS

Our experience with NodeJS


Working with NodeJS is very simple and straightforward, even beginners can pick it up and get started within minutes.
Setting up NodeJS is also extremely simple, and easily met our expectations for a backend framework.
